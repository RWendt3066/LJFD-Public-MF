WELCOME

Lake Johanna Fire Department, organized in 1943, is a private non-profit organization providing fire protection services to the Cities of Arden Hills, North Oaks, and Shoreview, Minnesota. The Department covers 36 square miles and protects a population of about 41,000 residents.

On behalf of the dedicated firefighters serving Lake Johanna Fire Department, I would like to thank you for visiting our web site.
